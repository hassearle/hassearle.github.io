Sourcing

Why Bulgaria?

Bulgaria is an agricultural haven for growing aromatic plants and the people are rich in expertise. Near the Black Sea, the sunny and dry climate coupled with the dry, well-drained, sandy soil make the growing conditions in Bulgaria ideal.

Why France?

France is famous for producing exceptionally high quality lavender. Some historians estimate that lavender production in France dates back as far as 600 BC..
